UCSF ChimeraX Toolshed

The UCSF ChimeraX Toolshed provides a convenient way of delivering (molecular) tools to ChimeraX users. Developers can sign up to submit applications and users can easily find them.

Technology

The UCSF ChimeraX Toolshed is based on the Cytoscape App Store. Please cite it if the UCSF ChimeraX Toolshed has been useful for your work. Citations help them secure funding in the future.
Lotia S, Montojo J, Dong Y, Bader GD, Pico AR. Cytoscape app store. Bioinformatics. 2013 May 15;29(10):1350-1.
